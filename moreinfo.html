About Page

This website is about one of my favorite hobbies: baseball. I began playing baseball when I was about seven, before the kids actually pitched. I thought that it was boring, so I convinced my family to let me play another sport. Eventually, I got back with baseball when I was about 13 and haven't looked back. I played for all four years on the Madison Academic High School team. Even though I am done with high school baseball, I will still enjoy watching baseball both on TV and in the stands. It will always be a passion for me, whether playing it, watching it, or coaching it someday.
